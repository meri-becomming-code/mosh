# MOSH ADA Toolkit - Getting Started (2026 Edition) 🤖♿

MOSH (Making Online Spaces Helpful) is an all-in-one accessibility suite designed to help educators meet the April 2026 ADA compliance deadline with ease.

## 🏁 How to Start
1.  **Launch the Toolkit**: Double-click the `MOSH_ADA_Toolkit_RCxx.exe` file.
2.  **One-Click Setup**: Go to the **Connect & Setup** tab.
3.  **Install Poppler**: Click the **"Auto-Setup Poppler"** button. This installs the "eyes" the toolkit needs to read Math and PDF content.
4.  **Add your AI Key (Optional)**: If you want to use the automated descriptions and Math OCR, enter your Gemini API key in the settings field.

## 🚀 The Mastery Workflow
MOSH is designed around a three-step workflow that ensures 100% course health:

### Step 0: Math & PDF Conversion (Start here!)
If your course has Math equations or PDFs, go to the **Math Converter** tab first. Process your documents here before moving to bulk remediation.

### Step 1: Automated "Auto-Fix"
Load your `.imscc` file and click **"Auto-Fix Issues"**. This uses an intelligent engine to batch-correct headings, table structures, and color contrast across your entire course in seconds.

### Step 2: Visual & Guided Review
Click **"Guided Review"** to walk through your course page-by-page. 
- Use **🤖 Auto-Describe** to let the AI write your alt-text.
- Use **📊 Table OCR** for pictures of data tables.
- Use **📝 Long Descriptions** for complex calculus or science diagrams.

### Step 3: Mirror & Sync
Turn on **"Mirror Mode"** to see your changes locally as you work. Once finished, upload your remediated package back to a Canvas sandbox for testing.
