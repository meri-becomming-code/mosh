## üé® PRODUCTION NOTES

### Equipment Needed:
- **Screen recording**: OBS Studio (free) or Camtasia
- **Camera**: Webcam or phone camera (doesn't need to be fancy)
- **Mic**: Built-in is fine, but a $30 USB mic is better
- **Editing**: DaVinci Resolve (free) or Camtasia

### Recording Tips:
1. **Screen recording first**: Record all the Canvas & toolkit parts BEFORE filming yourself
2. **Script on teleprompter**: Use free teleprompter apps so you sound natural
3. **Multiple takes OK**: You'll edit together the best parts
4. **Smile!**: Your enthusiasm is contagious

### Editing Checklist:
- [ ] Trim dead time (waiting, loading screens)
- [ ] Add callout boxes highlighting key steps
- [ ] Add background music (soft, non-distracting)
- [ ] Add captions/subtitles (accessibility!)
- [ ] Zoom in on important UI elements
- [ ] Keep it fast-paced (no section longer than 40 seconds)

### Resolution & Format:
- **Resolution**: 1920x1080 (1080p)
- **Format**: MP4
- **Upload to**: YouTube, LinkedIn, Vimeo
